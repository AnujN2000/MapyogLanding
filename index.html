MapYog

MapYog is dedicated to monitoring and understanding critical climate and weather metrics, like AQI and temperature, at a hyperlocal level. By curating data from multiple sources and using AI-driven techniques, HoodLab offers detailed spatial and temporal analysis, future predictions, and interactive map-based visualizations. Explore and interpret complex environmental data with ease, empowering you to make informed decisions for a healthier environment.

  • Learn more

HoodLab is built on Kepler.gl, leveraging its robust geospatial visualization capabilities while extending them to offer more customizable features. The use of deck.gl for creating layers allows HoodLab to handle complex visualizations, including 3D data representation and dynamic updates. This combination of technologies ensures that HoodLab can provide detailed, high-quality visualizations while offering the flexibility and scalability required for advanced geospatial analysis.
